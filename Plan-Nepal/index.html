Nepal Transformation 2035

A Comprehensive Development Blueprint for Sustainable Growth and Prosperity

Our National Vision

By 2035, Nepal will emerge as a developed nation through strategic investments in human capital, infrastructure, and technology, while preserving our rich cultural heritage and natural environment. This comprehensive plan outlines our path to sustainable prosperity.

Energy Independence

Generate 15,000 MW of clean hydroelectric power, achieving energy surplus and regional export capacity.

2,100 MW Current Capacity
15,000 MW 2035 Target

Education Revolution

Universal quality education with 100% literacy rate and STEM-focused curriculum nationwide.

72% Current Literacy
100% 2035 Target

Economic Growth

Sustain 8%+ annual GDP growth through diversified economy and increased FDI.

$36B Current GDP
$150B 2035 Target
